Ecobards

Geri İleri

Ecofences are a tool to retain floating garbage that is dragged by rivers into Lake Ilopango, in order to reduce pollution.

In 2021, the first 120-meter ecofence was installed, collecting more than 4 tons of recyclable and non-recyclable waste, preventing it from entering Lake Ilopango.
