Who are we?

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable development in Lake Ilopango. We have more than 28 years of experience developing environmental, social and economic projects in search of the preservation of biodiversity and the benefit of the communities surrounding the watershed. and the benefit of the communities surrounding the watershed.

Why are we important?

We encourage the participation of communities in the change of practices that pollute the practices that pollute the Ilopango Lake and the promotion of social social groups that guarantee continuity and sustainability for the benefit of the benefit of the hydrographic basin. With our programs we have have benefited approximately 220,000 people in 10 municipalities bordering the lake. that border the lake.

Why is Lake Ilopango important?

It is the largest lake in the country and the only aquatic lung of San Salvador. In its vicinity coexists a wide biodiversity and an average of 1 million people benefited, directly and indirectly. It is a national and international heritage due to its historical impact and volcanic nature.

What sets us apart?

We are the only NGO totally dedicated to the welfare of Lake Ilopango and its surroundings.

We raise awareness among the Salvadoran population about the importance of Lake Ilopango as a national heritage site.

Our strategic reforestation in several areas of the Ilopango Lake basin.

The creation of the Panoramic Route and the School of Tourist Guides to encourage sustainable development in the communities.

Our implementation of continuous cleaning days in the watershed
