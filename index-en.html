LAGO DE ILOPANGO

The great lung of San Salvador



What do we do?

We preserve the well-being of Lake Ilopango through:

  • Creation of the Panoramic Route
  • Development of a School of Tourist Guides
  • Organization of Continuous Cleaning Campaigns in the hydrographic basin
  • Strategic reforestation in the surrounding communities
  • Organization and development of the project "Jewels of Lake Ilopango"
  • Installation of echo fences
Eligibility requirements

In order to be part of and be a beneficiary you only need:


Living in the municipalities surrounding Lake Ilopango (MUNICIPALITIES OF THE BASIN)

Our focus on quality

Our focus is to make the population aware of the importance of Lake Ilopango as a national heritage, In addition, all our projects are aimed at protecting the biodiversity of Lake Ilopango and supporting the inhabitants of the basin.

Join us and protect the Lago de Ilopango

2,000 Trees provided

During 2018 we have worked hard in the development of various projects such as: reforestation program, in which more than 2,000 trees of various species were delivered to the municipalities.
